About

Where it all came from...


The story:

SQuelized was born out of GraphApp, a stock visualizing app made with Processing. This was a Junior Freshman Computer Science project done during our first year of Computer Science at Trinity College Dublin. During this project, I was in charge of implementing the API to connect to the MySQL database storing the stocks data. Given the lack of up-to-date libraries to facilitate the connection to SQL libraries, I was were forced to implement my own. This project was born to fill that gap and allow future students and learners to easily access the world of SQL databases through Processing. This is a growing project, so keep up with our latest updates!



Author - Samuel Alarco

I am student of Computer Science at Trinity College University of Dublin. I am passionate about programming and education, and love to code and work on personal projects such as this one to help other people learn about programming. I am also Co-founder and director of Solo Lattices, a company dedicated to the generation and implementation of lattice structures for 3D printing through our own software. I hope that you find this project useful!



Project Bio

This project was born out of a Junior Freshman Computer Science group project involving Processing visualizations and databases. Having found no up-to-date libraries that could help us implement a SQL database API for our project, we were forced to do everything from scratch. This is an effort to avoid other students going through the same troubles. Enjoy and learn!
